Rotary International

Rotary's legacy of compassion: Leaving footprints of impact on humanity.

Rotary International is a global humanitarian service organization with a mission to bring together business and professional leaders to provide humanitarian service, promote high ethical standards in all vocations, and advance goodwill and peace worldwide. Here are some key points about Rotary International: Rotary International focuses on seven main areas of service, known as the "Areas of Focus." These areas include promoting peace and conflict resolution, providing clean water and sanitation, supporting maternal and child health, improving basic education and literacy, promoting economic and community development, and fighting disease (including the global eradication of polio).protecting environment Rotary International has been at the forefront of the global effort to eradicate polio. In 1985, Rotary launched its Polio Plus program, aiming to immunize children against polio worldwide. Through partnerships with organizations like the World Health Organization (WHO) and UNICEF, Rotary has made giant/significant progress in reducing polio cases globally. The Rotary Foundation is the charitable arm of Rotary International. It supports Rotary's humanitarian efforts by providing grants and funding for projects and initiatives. The Foundation also offers scholarships, vocational training, and exchange programs. Rotary International provides a platform for networking, professional development, and fellowship among its members. Rotarians have opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals, attend conferences and events, and build international friendships. The Four-Way Test is a guiding principle of Rotary International. It is a set of ethical guidelines Rotarians are encouraged to apply to their personal and professional lives. Rotary International continues to play a vital role in addressing global challenges and making a positive impact on communities around the world. For more detailed and up-to-date information, I recommend visiting the official Rotary International website.
